Ikarus 280


This is Ikarus 280.03, which was produced by a legendary Hungarian bus manufacturer Ikarus

Similarly to Ikarus 260, the mass production started in 1973 (1 year after Ikarus 260) and finished in 2002 (exactly the same year as Ikarus 260). Ikarus 280 is an articulated bus (AKA accordion bus), which makes it quite an interesting vehicle. It also means, that Ikarus 280 can fit a lot more people than traditional bus designs: up to ~160 passengers. That's more than twice the amount of passengers Ikarus 260 can fit

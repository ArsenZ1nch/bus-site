Ikarus 260


This is Ikarus 260, which was produced by a legendary Hungarian bus manufacturer Ikarus

Ikarus 260 was manufactured from 1972 to 2002, although the first prototypes were finished in 1970. The city model of Ikarus 260 can fit 21 sitting and ~76 standing passengers

Although Ikarus officially ceased to exist in 2000s, it still has a few successors which continue to manufacture busses
